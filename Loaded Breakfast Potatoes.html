Loaded Breakfast Potatoes

Crispy potatoes topped with bacon, cheese, and tangy green onions for a yummy meal.



Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • olive oil
  • 2 medium Potatoes
  • 1/2 Onion, chopped
  • Bacon Strips
  • 1/4 green onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp of salt



How do you make it?

  1. Heat your oven to 220 C

  2. In a pan combine all of your ingredients

  3. Bake for 30 minutes, flip every 10 minutes
