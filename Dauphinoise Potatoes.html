Dauphinoise Potatoes

A french classic potato meal that bound to make your day feel a lot more better!



Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • thyme
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of cream



How do you make it?

  1. Cut your potatoes thinly and in a buttered tray stack them evenly.

  2. Mix your cream, garllic, theyme, salt and pepper then pour it over the sliced potatoes.

  3. Top it with cheese.

  4. Cook in your preheated oven at 250 C for 20-25 minutes

  5. Serve!
