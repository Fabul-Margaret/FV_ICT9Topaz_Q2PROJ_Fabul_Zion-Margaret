Smashed Roasties

A yummy twist on your favorite roasted potatoes.



Ingredients

  • baby potatoes
  • Seasonings of your liking
  • 1/4 cup butter



How do you make it?

  1. Boil your potatoes in hot water, it sould be soft enough to be flattend but not mashed

  2. In a buttered pan place your potatoes and sprinkle with your seasonings

  3. Bake for 200 C for 10-20 Minutes

  4. Serve!
