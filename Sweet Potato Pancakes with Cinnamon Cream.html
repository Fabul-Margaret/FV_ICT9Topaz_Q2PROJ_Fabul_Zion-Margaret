Sweet Potato Pancakes with Cinnamon Cream

Pancakes made from sweet potatoes topped wiith a cinnamon cream syrup.



Ingredients

  • Pancake Part:
  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking power
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup sweet potato puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp of melted butter
  • Cinnamon Cream Part:
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon



How do you make it?

  1. In a bowl mix all of your dry ingredients togther

  2. In an another bowl mix all of your wet ingredients then incoporate that to the bowl with your dry ingredients

  3. In a seperate smaller bowl, to make your syrup mix all of your ingredients together

  4. Fry your batter until done

  5. serve with the syrup
