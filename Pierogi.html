Pierogi

Dumpling filled with a tender, and cheesy potato filling topped with buttery sauteed onions



Ingredients

  • Pierogi Filling:
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 3 large Onions
  • 1 1/2 cups large curd cottage cheese
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 5 poatoes
  • Pierogi Dough:
  • 4 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs



How do you make it?

  1. To make the dough mix all of the dough ingredients together.

  2. While your dough settles in another bowl mix all of your filling ingredients.

  3. Wrap your meat with the dough

  4. Boil your Pierogi

  5. Serve!
