Southwestern Hash with Eggs

A quick and easy hardy meal packed with protein to start off your day.



Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 pork steak, cubed
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 3 Large eggs



How do you make it?

  1. Cook pork in oil in an ovenproof skillet for about 4 minutes, or until browned. For four minutes, saute and mix the potato, onion, garlic, and green pepper. Add the cumin, tomatoes, bouillon, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Cook, covered, over low heat, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are soft, about 30 minutes. Break an egg into each of the four wells created in the hash. The eggs should be fully set after 10 to 12 minutes of baking at 350°, uncovered.
