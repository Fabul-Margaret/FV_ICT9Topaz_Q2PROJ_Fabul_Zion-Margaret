Cepelinai

Dumplings made from potato dough and is stuffed with delicious meat, cheese, and vegetables.



Ingredients

  • Cepelinai Filling:
  • 1 lb of minced pork
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Pierogi Dough:
  • 6 lb of potatoes
  • 2 tsp salt
  • Potato starch/cornflour



How do you make it?

  1. Boil your potatoes, mash, mix the dry ingredients for you to form a dough.

  2. In a seperate bowl mix all of your filling ingredients

  3. Wrap your filling with the dough

  4. Boil your Cepelinai until cooked

  5. Serve!
