Japanese Potato Salad

A light potato salad to end your day on a refreshing note



Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • A carrot
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • Cooked ham



How do you make it?

  1. Boil your potatoes and carrots, add salt to taste until both are tender and can be mashed

  2. Cut your cooked ham and cucumber thinly

  3. To assemble mix your vegetables with the ham and cucumber

  4. Serve!
