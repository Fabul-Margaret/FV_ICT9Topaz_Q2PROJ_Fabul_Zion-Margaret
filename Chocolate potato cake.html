Chocolate potato cake

A chocolaty treat made with potatoes!?



Ingredients

  • 4 big potatoes
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 3/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 sweetend cocoa powder
  • 1/2 of unsalted butter, browm for more flavour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt



How do you make it?

  1. Preheat your oven tp 180 C

  2. Peel and shread your potatoes

  3. In a seperate bowl prepare your cake mixture, mix in the wet and dry ingredients

  4. Add your potatoes

  5. In a buttered pan, pour in your mixture and bake for 35-50 minutes

  6. Serve!
