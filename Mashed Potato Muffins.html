Mashed Potato Muffins

Mashed potatoes... But make them crispy!?



Ingredients

  • 3 cups of mashed potatoes
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup of grated cheese



How do you make it?

  1. Preheat your oven to 182 C

  2. In a large bowl mix all of ingridients.

  3. Bake the miture into the oven for 30-35 minutes until crispy

  4. Serve!
